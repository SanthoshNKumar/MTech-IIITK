Ex: Mutually Exclusive

S = {1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8}

A = {2,5,7}
B = {1,3,4,6,8}

P(A U B) = P(A) + P(B) 
         = {1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8}

********************************************************

Ex: Not Mutually Exclusive

A = {2,3,5,7}
B = {1,3,4,6,8}

P(A U B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B)

P(A) + P(B) = {1,2,3,3,4,5,6,7,8}

P(A ∩ B) = {3}

= P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B) 

= {1,2,3,3,4,5,6,7,8} - {3}

= {1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8}
